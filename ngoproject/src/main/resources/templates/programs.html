Our Programs

Coding on Wheels

The "Coding on Wheels" project is a transformative initiative of Global Technopower Foundation (GTPF) dedicated to empowering the most vulnerable and often overlooked members of our society: Destitute students in orphanage. These children face unique challenges, and our project is committed to bridging the digital divide for them. With a mobile learning unit equipped with cutting-edge technology and expert instructors, we take the world of coding and computer science directly to the doorsteps of orphanages. By providing interactive coding lessons, workshops, and engaging activities, we introduce these students to the exciting world of technology.

Women Empowerment

We aim to championing a transformative mission of empowering women and girls to reclaim their lives from the clutches of monthly discomfort and pain. Our intervention is simple yet profound - providing access to Mihir organic rice, a natural remedy with the potential to alleviate menstrual agony. Beyond mere distribution, we are igniting a movement. We conduct awareness programs, nurturing health and hygiene education, equipping women and girls with the knowledge to manage their menstrual health confidently.
