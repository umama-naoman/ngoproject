Who We Are

Glob TechPower Foundation (GTPF) is a non profit organisation driven by a shared vision. A vision that arose out of diverse backgrounds and experiences of professionals who came together with a common goal to use technology and digital innovation for positive and constructive educational, social as well as community impact. We believe in the potential of technology and it’s ability to transform lives and uplift communities from the underserve and marginalize background. This united commitment sparked the birth of Global Technopower Foundation (GTPF).

Our Vision

We believe that no one should be left behind in the digital edge thus we envisage a society in which underserved communities are empowered, educated, and economically self-sufficient, thus breaking the cycle of poverty and inequalities

Our Mission

To bring our vision to reality, we have established a following mission that informs all of our actions: “Empowering Communities Through Innovations and Technology”

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: Diversity and inclusivity are values we uphold in all facets of our work. We ensure that all community members, regardless of gender and socioeconomic background, have equal access to opportunities and resources.
  • Empowerment: We strive to empower individuals and communities belonging to underserved and marginalised background to take control of their own purposes by providing them with the necessary tools, knowledge and resources they need to thrive in the digital age.
  • Adaptability: We are flexible and responsive to the evolving needs and challenges of the community, adjusting strategies as necessary.
  • Collaboration: We believe that collaboration and working together is the foremost value. Thus, we collaborate with partners, corporates, governments, volunteers, and communities to achieve shared goals.
  • Ethical Conduct: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all interactions and operations, ensuring the welfare of the community remains paramount.

Our Approach

Our approach is comprehensive, spanning various aspects of technology and digital solutions. We focus on digital literacy and education, access to technology and supporting innovation. Whether it is about offering digital skills training and education to underprivileged, gender equality, youth empowerment or any other social impact initiatives or programs of our organisation. We approach each task with passion and determination.
