Padho Bhi Padhao Bhi

Together, Let's Padho Bhi Padhao Bhi!

We are delighted to introduce the "Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi" campaign, a noble initiative aimed at transforming the lives of orphanage students by providing them with the invaluable gift of coding education under our project ‘Coding on Wheels’. Recognizing the power of education in fostering self-independence and a dignified life, we are reaching out to compassionate individuals, corporates, and well-to-do families to join hands in supporting this meaningful cause.

How you can contribute?

  • Financial Support: Your generous contributions will directly fund coding education programs for orphanage students. Every donation, regardless of size, plays a crucial role in shaping a brighter future for these young minds.
  • Spread the Word: Share the campaign across your networks and social media platforms. By raising awareness, we can garner broader support and reach our fundraising goals sooner.
  • Corporate Sponsorship: We invite corporates to become partners in this noble endeavor. Corporate sponsorships can significantly impact the scale and reach of the coding education programs, creating a lasting positive effect on the lives of orphanage students.

Corporate Training for Social Impact

"Shaping Futures: Join Us to Empower Orphan and Government School Students!"

Welcome to "Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi", a trailblazing campaign that seamlessly integrates corporate growth with social responsibility. In this unique initiative, we present a golden opportunity for your company and employees to partake in transformative technology training while contributing to a noble cause.

How It Works?

Engage your employees in cutting-edge technology training, and in return, we request a minimum donation of 5000 rupees per participant. The accumulated donations will be directed towards providing coding education to orphanage students and underprivileged children in government schools, fostering a brighter and more technologically inclusive future.

Why Engage?

  • Dual Impact: Your team's professional development aligns seamlessly with a philanthropic endeavor. Each training session becomes a gateway to personal growth for employees and a lifeline of opportunity for those in need.
  • Social Empowerment: By contributing to the coding education of orphanage students and underprivileged youth, your company becomes a driving force in empowering marginalized communities, breaking barriers to education and fostering digital literacy.
  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Amplified: "Padho aur Padhne Do" not only complements your CSR initiatives but enhances them.
  • Collective Progress: This campaign encourages a collective approach to progress. As your team advances in tech proficiency, their journey becomes intertwined with the aspirations of those yearning for educational opportunities.
